Tonga

Tonga is a Polynesian kingdom made up of more than 170 islands scattered across the South Pacific. Known as the “Friendly Islands,” it is rich in culture, traditions, and natural beauty. The capital, Nukuʻalofa, is located on the main island of Tongatapu. Tonga is famous for its pristine beaches, vibrant coral reefs, and strong cultural heritage, including traditional dance, music, and feasts.
